Two Cats Records

For this project, we had to conceptualize a design for a music magazine of any genre. I made mine about indie music that melts genres together. Two Cats Records is where past meets future in the precious present. It's where soul and folk that pours from the depth of your stomach meets the brainy waves of electronic pop and floats up to the stars. It's where art helps us imagine a future where nature, humanity and technology all live together in peace.

Down here you can see the playlist I came up with and listened to while I worked, as well as the image that inspired my cover.

Green Bird Studio

I made this logo for my mom in 2022 for her piano studio, Green Bird Studio. She wanted something whimsical and organic, that would help her convey the idea of making music for the love of it, not for perfection. I helped her sum up this spirit with the phrase "Music for the heart," and the base of this logo came from the very first sketch I made, which she loved.

Below you can see the progression of ideas through the variants of design. We put the last bird with no nest or circle on a hat.
