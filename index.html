About Me

I love to make things.

I have been making things my whole life, and I'm proud that I got to continue that into adulthood. Here, you'll see my extremly varied interests, from drawing to film to poetry to crochet.

When I'm not making things, I love to hang out with family, friends, and Harry and Pancake the scottish terriers, when they're not barking! I love symbolism, comedy, and I've had a lifelong back and forth with cheese. I'm also a Christian, and so I want to make work that does good things for people and their souls. Sometimes. Sometimes I just gotta be silly.
