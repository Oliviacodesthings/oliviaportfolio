I love to make coloring pages! I always loved coloring as a kid, and I still do. There's something so exciting about the millions of different ways to make a page beautiful. One hundred people will all color one coloring page differently, and in doing so they work with the artist to make something of their own! I love getting to give people my work and seeing how they interpret it.

When I make a page, I start with a freehand drawing on paper. Then I scan it and make edits in photoshop. Finally, I use Adobe Illustrator's image trace feature to stylize and give the page its final form.
